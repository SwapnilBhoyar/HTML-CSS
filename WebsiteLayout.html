Covid-19: India reports 62,480 new cases, 1,587 deaths in last 24 hours

At 1,587, India reports least daily Covid deaths in 60 days

Meanwhile, the country's active caseload has fallen to 7,98,656, which is below 8 lakh after 73 days. With 88, 977 patients recovered during the last 24 hours, daily recoveries have overtaken the number of daily cases for the 36th consecutive day. The daily positivity rate stands at 3.24%, which is less than 5% for 11 consecutive days.
